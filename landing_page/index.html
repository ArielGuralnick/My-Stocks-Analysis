I
§ 01 · WORKFLOW

The three-step filter.

One script. Three layers of scrutiny. No manual trading. No mock data. Every signal must survive technicals, fundamentals, and cooldown.

  1. 01

    EXCEL INGEST · TECHNICAL FILTER

    Four indicators.
    Confluence required.

    Loads your portfolio straight from an Excel export. Pulls two years of daily OHLCV from Yahoo Finance and evaluates four independent indicators — SMA 200 RSI 14 MACD BB 20/2 — on both the BUY and SELL sides. A signal only fires when at least three of four confirm each other.

  2. 02

    AI FUNDAMENTAL LAYER · CLAUDE SONNET 4.6

    Claude reads the news.
    You get the verdict.

    When the technical filter triggers, the system pulls the five most recent headlines via yfinance and hands them to Claude. Claude returns a strict JSON verdict — BUY, SELL, or HOLD — with a concise three-sentence fundamental analysis. Raw headlines never touch your phone.

  3. 03

    REAL-TIME WHATSAPP ALERTS · GREEN API

    One message.
    Everything you need.

    A fully-formatted alert lands in WhatsApp with the indicators that fired, the AI's take, and a flag when the two disagree. A 48-hour per-ticker cooldown keeps you out of spam territory. The alert goes out whether or not Claude agrees with the technicals — you decide.
